About the Conference

What Is Marriage on a Mission?

Marriage on a Mission is a 2-day transformational experience designed to restore, strengthen, and activate your marriage. Whether you're in a season of struggle or simply want to go deeper, this conference meets you exactly where you are.

We'll spend Friday evening together over dinner and an opening session laying the biblical foundation for marriage. Then on Saturday, we go all the way — five powerful sessions, honest conversations, practical tools, and a closing covenant experience you won't forget.

This isn't a lecture. This is an encounter. Come ready to be honest, to be healed, and to leave with a vision for your marriage that outlasts you.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do both spouses need to register separately? ▼

No — registration is per couple. One registration covers both spouses. Please include both names in the form so we can prepare your materials.

What is included in the registration fee? ▼

Your registration includes Friday welcome dinner, Saturday breakfast and lunch, all session materials and participant workbooks, and the Saturday closing celebration dinner. Lodging is separate and booked through the room reservation link above.

What if our marriage is in a really difficult place right now? ▼

Then you're exactly who this conference was built for. Many couples come in crisis and leave with hope, tools, and a renewed commitment. The environment is safe, confidential, and led with care. You don't need to have it together to come — just come.

What is the cancellation and refund policy? ▼

Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the event (September 25, 2025). After that date, registrations are non-refundable but may be transferred to another couple. Contact us at info.nwfle@gmail.com for any changes.

Do we have to stay on-site? ▼

On-site lodging is optional but highly recommended. Staying on-site lets you fully immerse without the distraction of commuting, and gives you quiet time together in the evenings.
